    Interesting Facts About Teddy Bears!

Interesting Facts About Pandas!

  • Adult pandas can eat around 12-38 kilos of bamboo every day!
  • Pandas are quite good at climbing trees and can also swim!
  • Pandas have carnivorous teeth, but usually only eat bamboo and fruit!

Interesting Facts About Pigeons!

  • Pigeons can fly at an altitude of 6000 feet!
  • The fastest pigeon reached the speed of 92 mph!
